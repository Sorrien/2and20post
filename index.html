The talented duo behind 2 and 20 Post has been delivering excellence in post-production for 20+ years.

Todd and Carolyn first collaborated while finishing the “I, Robot” teaser for 20th Century Fox - Todd was working with the creative team at mOcean and Carolyn was Director of Film-Finishing at Picture Head.

Their career paths have crossed off and on since then, but now they have joined their post-production forces to form 2 and 20 Post.
